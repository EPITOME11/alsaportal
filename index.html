WELCOME TO ALSA

The African Law Students' Association

ALSA is a premier body for African Law Students and Lawyers. It renders an E-learn platform for Open and Distance Education since 2020.

  • Connect with thousands of law students and experienced legal practitioners world wide.
  • Improve in your career and chances of being hired by reputable clients by joining ALSA community.
  • Take professional courses from ALSA E-Learn to acquaint your professionalism in legal practices and broaden your career diversity as a lawyer.

Our exclusive E-Library gives you access to unlimited legal papers for academic, career building and reseach purposes.

ALSA Membership Benefits

Becoming an ALSA member gives you the title 'MLSA' automatically to be attached to your official name. You stand the chance of free access to all ALSA legal materials, free E-Learn and professional exams. Attend ALSA National and International conferences and be entitled for presentations and award winning. Be hired by ALSA clients regularly for solicitation and advocacy. Publish your legal research papers in ALSA Journal. Connect and share ideas with fellow law students and practicing lawyers globally.

Become a Member

Individual Membership

This is a membership category for individuals of african nations that are studying law or related course to law or are lawyers by profession.

Institutional Membership

Students of faculty or department of law of any high institution can apply for institutional membership in the name of such institutions through a leader.

International Membership

Students of law or lawyers from continents other than africa can become a member through international membership application procedures.

Popular Courses

ACCOUNTING LAW

$12.00

Start Course

Accounting, often known as profit accounting, is a system for recording, summarizing, analyzing, and categorizing a person's or company's financial activities. We offer Law Professional Certificate (LPC) program on this course for law students & graduates.

Facilitator: Charles Ofodum

MARITIME LAW

$12.00

Start Course

Marine law, (or admiralty law) is a set of rules, conventions, and treaties that govern private maritime enterprise and other nautical affairs like shipping and open-water offenses. Acquire an LPC now and broaden your knowledge in Maritime treaties by enrolling into this course.

Facilitator: A. James

LAW OF EQUITY

$12.00

Start Course

ALSA School of Legal Studies unveils Law of Equity (Equity Law) as a Law Professional Certificate (LPC) Course. Do you want to know more about law equitable remedies, principles of equity, equitable maxims and origin of law? If yes, this program is for you.

Facilitator: Prince Ulame
